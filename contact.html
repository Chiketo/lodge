Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I book a room?

You can book directly through our website using the "Book Now" form, or contact our reservations team via phone or email for assistance.

❓ What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 7 days before check-in. Cancellations within 7 days may incur charges. See our booking page for full terms.

❓ Do you offer WiFi?

Yes, free high-speed WiFi is available in all rooms and common areas throughout the lodge.

❓ What time are check-in and check-out?

Standard check-in is 2:00 PM and check-out is 10:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out available upon request.

❓ Do you have facilities for disabled guests?

Yes, we have wheelchair accessible rooms and facilities. Please inform us of any special needs when booking.

❓ Is breakfast included?

Some room packages include breakfast. Continental breakfast is available for an additional charge ($15/person). Check your booking details.

❓ Do you organize events or conferences?

Yes! We have conference facilities and can organize events. Contact our events team for customized packages and pricing.

❓ Are pets allowed?

We welcome well-behaved pets with advance notice. Please contact us before booking to arrange pet accommodations.

Still Have Questions?

Our friendly team is here to help. Don't hesitate to reach out!
