Welcome to Our Lodge

Nestled in the heart of Mutare, Zimbabwe, Forest Creek Lodge is a premier destination for travelers seeking authentic African hospitality combined with modern comfort. Our lodge was founded in 2015 with a vision to provide guests with an unforgettable experience surrounded by nature's beauty.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence in service, sustainable tourism practices, and creating memories that last a lifetime. Whether you're visiting for business, leisure, or a special celebration, our dedicated team is here to ensure your stay exceeds expectations.
